Full English Recipe

Ingredients

A yummy full English breakfast with all the extras!

  • 4 sausages
  • 2 tomatoes, halved
  • 300 g pack mini Portabella mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 8 rashers smoked back bacon
  • 3 slices white bread, cut into triangles
  • 4 slices black pudding
  • 400 g baked beans
  • 4 medium free-range eggs

Steps

  1. Warm the beans in a small pot over low heat.
  2. Cook sausages in a pan until browned. Push aside and fry the bacon.
  3. Add and fry black pudding if using. Keep everything warm.
  4. In another pan, sear mushrooms until browned, then sear tomatoes cut-side down.
  5. Fry bread in the meat drippings and cook eggs in the vegetable pan.
