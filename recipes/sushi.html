Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

Salmon sushi with cream cheese and avocado!

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sashimi-grade raw salmon
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1 avocado
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Lay a bamboo mat flat and have water nearby.
  2. Place nori shiny side down on the mat.
  3. Spread rice as an even layer on nori.
  4. Place filling on nori sheets.
  5. Use the mat to roll and seal the nori.
  6. Wet a sharp knife and cut into 6 to 8 pieces.
  7. Enjoy immediately with soy sauce.
